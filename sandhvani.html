What is Sandhvani?

The Ultimate Media Showcase

Sandhvani '26 is MUONS' flagship media competition and celebration day, bringing together creative minds from across the Island to compete in Announcing, Dubbing, Sports commentary, Technical and more.

Multiple Categories

Announcing, Dubbing, Sports commentary, Technical and more

Competition Categories

Choose Your Battleground

Select your category and showcase your skills. Each category has specific requirements and judging criteria.

Announcing

A prestige event conducted in English and Sinhala across Junior, Intermediate, and Senior categories. Deliver a flawless performance using official scripts.

  • Max 5 minutes duration
  • Single continuous take (No editing)
  • School uniform required
  • Submit by midnight 10th May
Junior, Intermediate, and Senior

Dubbing

Compete in Sinhala or English for Junior or Senior categories. Use provided clips or select your own to demonstrate vocal synchronization.

  • Contestant & video both visible
  • Strictly unedited video format
  • School uniform required
  • Submit by midnight 10th May
Junior and Senior

Program Presenting

An online competition in Sinhala and English for Junior and Senior categories. Present a professional program segment based on provided scripts.

  • Max 6 minutes duration
  • High audio/visual quality required
  • Single continuous MP4 submission
  • Submit by midnight 10th May
Junior and Senior

Sports Commentary

An open-category event in Sinhala and English. Provide live-style commentary for Cricket, Netball, Football, Athletics, or Rugby.

  • Contestant & video both visible
  • Continuous and unedited recording
  • School uniform required
  • Submit by midnight 10th May
Open

Editing

A handwritten competition for Junior, Intermediate, and Senior categories. Scan or photograph manual scripts and compile into a single document.

  • Strict 2-3 hour time duration
  • Must be handwritten (No AI)
  • Submit as single PDF document
  • Deadline: 12 PM/1 PM
Junior, Intermediate, and Senior

Technical Paper

Strictly English-medium event for Open Category. This entirely paperless competition requires independent work within a fixed time frame.

  • 1.5 hours duration
  • Google Form submission
  • AI tools strictly prohibited
  • Single submission only
Open

Graphic Designing

Open Category competition where participants create digital designs based on specific themes using professional software without AI.

  • Submit as .jpg or .png file
  • Must be entirely original work
  • AI-generated designs disqualified
  • Submit by 16th May 2026
Open

Photography

Open Category divided into Color, Monochrome, and Mobile subcategories. Capture high-quality images to showcase your visual perspective.

  • Max 3 photos per subcategory
  • No drones, watermarks, or manipulation
  • RAW images may be requested
  • Submit by 16th May 2025
Open

Cartoon Drawing

Open Category event focusing on manual artistic skill. Receive specific topics and use any coloring medium to bring illustrations to life.

  • Must be on A4 size paper
  • Any coloring medium allowed
  • Upload photo of drawing by link
  • Submit before 1:00 PM
Open

Short Film Competition

The premier Open Category team event for any cinematic theme. The only category that allows participants to also compete in other events.

  • Max 10 minutes duration
  • Maximum of 10 participants per team
  • Submission via Google Form
  • Submit within two weeks (By May 23rd)
Open
