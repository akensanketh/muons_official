What is Sandhvani?

The Ultimate Media Showcase

Sandhvani '26 is MUONS' flagship media competition and celebration day, bringing together creative minds from across the school to compete in photography, videography, graphic design, and content creation. It's a day where creativity meets competition, and stories come to life.

Multiple Categories

Photography, videography, graphic design, and more

Expert Judging

Industry professionals evaluating your work

Amazing Prizes

Trophies, certificates, and special recognition
